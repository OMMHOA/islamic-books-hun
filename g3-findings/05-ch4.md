## [FIX-BOTH] AR p.112 / HUN ~line 1880 / ENG ~line 1919

ENG: "'Āṣim ibn 'Umar ibn Qatāda **(رضي الله عنه)** spoke to me of what the elders of his tribe said."
HUN: "'Āṣim ibn 'Umar ibn Qatāda **(رضي الله عنه)** beszélt nekem arról, amit törzsének vénei mondtak"
→ 'Āṣim ibn 'Umar ibn Qatāda ibn al-Nu'mān al-Anṣārī (d. ~120 AH) is a well-known **Tābi'ī** historian of the *maghāzī* (a generation removed — he here reports what he heard *from the elders of his tribe*, i.e. he was not himself a witness), not a Ṣaḥābī. Recommend dropping the honorific in both ENG-full and HUN, consistent with the fidelity policy (honorifics only for actual Ṣaḥābah/prophets stay; this one doesn't qualify and should be corrected like the Mūsā ibn 'Uqbah case).

## [NOTE] AR p.109 footnote¹ / HUN fn¹ ~line 2230 / ENG fn¹ ~line 2269

## [FIX-BOTH] AR p.131 / HUN ~line 2176 / ENG ~line 2215

Same error class as ENGLISH-EDITION-ERRATA.md §4 (corrupted names). Present in both ENG-full and HUN; recommend correcting to "Banū 'Amr ibn 'Awf" / "Banu 'Amr ibn 'Auf" in both files.

Per the fidelity policy this is exactly the kind of English-edition gap that should be restored in both files: add Bilāl's couplet + gloss footnote, and restore the two missing ṣaḥīḥ footnote markers/citations for 'Ā'ishah's and Anas's reports.
